How to Prevent Your Child from Going Missing

Teach Safety Basics

  • Teach your child their full name, address, and phone number
  • Establish a safe word that only family members know
  • Teach them never to go anywhere with strangers
  • Practice 'what if' scenarios regularly

Supervision and Monitoring

  • Always supervise young children in public places
  • Know where your child is at all times
  • Establish check-in times when they're out
  • Know your child's friends and their parents

Documentation

  • Keep recent, clear photos of your child updated regularly
  • Record voice samples for identification purposes
  • Maintain a file with physical descriptions and medical info
  • Keep copies of important documents safe

Public Places Safety

  • Dress children in bright, noticeable clothing in crowds
  • Designate a meeting spot if you get separated
  • Take a photo of your child before entering crowded areas
  • Teach children to look for police or security if lost

Online Safety

  • Monitor your child's online activities
  • Teach them never to share personal information online
  • Know who they're communicating with online
  • Set parental controls on devices

Build Trust

  • Create an environment where children feel safe sharing concerns
  • Listen to your child's feelings and experiences
  • Encourage them to tell you if someone makes them uncomfortable
  • Never dismiss their concerns as unimportant

Success Stories - Hope Restored

Ahmed's Return

7-year-old Ahmed was separated from his family during a busy market visit in Cairo. His parents immediately filed a report on Returno, uploading recent photos. Three days later, our AI system matched Ahmed's face with footage from a bus station camera 200km away. Local authorities were notified, and Ahmed was safely returned to his overjoyed family.

Found in 3 days

Fatima's Reunion

65-year-old Fatima, who lives with memory challenges, wandered away from her home in Alexandria. Her family was devastated and immediately contacted Returno. After 14 days, a shelter worker uploaded an audio recording to our system. Our voice recognition AI matched it with samples provided by Fatima's daughter.

Found in 14 days

Omar's Journey Home

12-year-old Omar got lost while on a school trip in Luxor. His guardian filed a report with multiple photos and voice recordings. Five days later, Omar was spotted by a Good Samaritan who used our app to scan faces in the area. Within two hours of the match, Omar was reunited with his family.

Found in 5 days

Mariam's Safe Return

9-year-old Mariam was separated from her mother in a crowded shopping mall in Giza. Her mother uploaded photos immediately to Returno. Within 6 hours, our facial recognition identified Mariam in security footage from a nearby restaurant.

Found in 6 hours

Youssef's Recovery

15-year-old Youssef went missing after a family dispute. His parents filed a report with Returno, including photos and voice messages. After 10 days, a youth center worker recognized him from our database. Youssef was provided counseling and safely reunited with his family.

Found in 10 days

Nour's Miracle

4-year-old Nour disappeared from a park in Aswan. The family immediately used Returno to report her missing. After 8 days, a tourist uploaded a photo to social media that our system flagged. Nour was found safe with a family who had mistakenly thought she was abandoned.
